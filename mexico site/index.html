A Place on Earth

Mexico is a large and diverse nation located in the southern portion of North America.

  • Bordered by the United States to the north and Guatemala and Belize to the south.
  • The capital and largest metropolis is Mexico City.
  • Boasts extensive coastlines on the Pacific Ocean (west) and the Gulf of Mexico & Caribbean Sea (east).

Form of Government

Mexico is a federal presidential republic. The President acts as both head of state and head of government.

Current President: Claudia Sheinbaum

Executive

Led by the President, responsible for executing and enforcing the laws.

Legislative

Congress of the Union (Senate and Chamber of Deputies) creates laws.

Judicial

The Supreme Court of Justice interprets the law and ensures constitutionality.

Economic Powerhouse

Mexico possesses a large, diverse, and developing economy, strongly tied to its North American neighbors through trade agreements (like USMCA). It trades extensively with the United States and Canada.

Manufacturing

A global leader in producing automobiles, electronics, and aerospace components.

Agriculture

Major exporter of corn, beans, avocados, tomatoes, and coffee.

Tourism

One of the top visited countries worldwide, known for beaches and ancient ruins.

Rich Traditions

Mexican culture is famously rich, colorful, and traditional, heavily emphasizing the importance of family in society.

Día de los Muertos

The Day of the Dead is a beautiful celebration where families gather to honor and remember their loved ones who have passed away.

Mariachi Folkloric Dance Vivid Textiles Lively Festivals

World-Renowned Cuisine

UNESCO recognizes traditional Mexican food as an Intangible Cultural Heritage of Humanity. The cuisine revolves around staple ingredients: corn, beans, chili peppers, and avocado.
